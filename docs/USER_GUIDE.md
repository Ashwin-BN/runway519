# User Guide

**Runway-519 · See More. Sell Smarter.**

This guide is for store associates using Runway-519 on the floor.
No technical knowledge required.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Open Runway-519 on your phone or browser
2. Tap **Sign Up**
3. Enter your full name, work email, and a password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Tap **Create Account**
5. You are signed in as an **Associate**

Your store manager can upgrade your access level if you need to do markdowns or other tasks.

### Signing In

1. Enter your email and password
2. Tap **Sign In**
3. You land on the **Inventory** page

The app remembers your session — you stay signed in until you tap Sign Out.

---

## The Bottom Navigation

On your phone, four tabs appear at the bottom of the screen:

| Tab           | What it does                   |
| ------------- | ------------------------------ |
| **Inventory** | View and search all items      |
| **Add Item**  | Add a new item to the system   |
| **Analytics** | View store insights and charts |
| **Users**     | Manage users (Admin only)      |

---

## Adding an Item

Tap **Add Item** in the bottom navigation or the **Add** button at the top right of the Inventory page.

### Photos

You can add up to 4 photos per item.

- Tap **Take Photo** → opens your phone camera directly (rear camera, best for products)
- Tap **Gallery** → choose existing photos from your phone library
- The **first photo** is the cover image shown on item cards
- Tap the **×** on any photo to remove it
- Photos are automatically compressed — no need to resize anything beforehand

### Item Details

| Field         | What to enter                               | Example             |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Brand         | Brand name exactly as it appears on the tag | Calvin Klein        |
| Department    | Select the 2-digit code from the dropdown   | 04 — Men's Clothing |
| Category Code | 4-digit number from the item tag            | 1042                |
| Style Number  | 6-digit number from the item tag            | 123456              |

### Price

- Enter the price shown on the tag
- The price **must end in .99** (e.g. 49.99, 129.99, 299.99)
- Tap away from the field and it auto-formats for you

### Notes (Optional)

Add anything extra — item condition, floor location, colour details, or anything useful for your team.

### Saving

Tap **Add Item** to save. You are taken to the item's detail page.

## Browsing Inventory

The **Inventory** page shows all items currently in the system.

Each item card shows:

- Cover photo (or a placeholder icon if no photos were added)
- Brand name
- Department, category, and style number codes
- Price — with markdown price and savings badge if the item has been marked down
- Status badge (Active / Markdown / Sold / Archived)

Tap any card to open the full item detail.

---

## Searching and Filtering

**Search bar** — type any part of a brand name, style number, or department code. Results update instantly as you type.

**Filter button** — tap it to expand the filter panel:

| Filter     | What it does                                        |
| ---------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Department | Show only items from a specific dept code           |
| Status     | Show only Active, Markdown, Sold, or Archived items |
| Min Price  | Hide items below this price                         |
| Max Price  | Hide items above this price                         |
| Sort By    | Change the order — newest, price, or brand name     |

Active filters appear as small chips below the search bar.
Tap the **×** on any chip to remove just that filter.
Tap **Clear All** to reset everything at once.

The counter at the bottom of the filter bar shows "Showing X of Y items" and updates live.

---

## Item Detail Page

Tap any item card to open the full detail view.

### Photo Gallery

- Tap the left and right arrows to move between photos
- Tap the dots at the bottom to jump to a specific photo
- Tap the thumbnail strip to switch photos quickly

### Item Information

Shows brand, department, category, style number, original price, markdown price, savings amount, and any notes added.

### Activity History

Every change made to this item is recorded here — who added it, who edited it, and when each change happened.

---

## Item Statuses

| Status       | What it means                      |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| **Active**   | On the floor at full price         |
| **Markdown** | Price has been reduced             |
| **Sold**     | Has been purchased by a customer   |
| **Archived** | No longer active, kept for records |

---

## Dark and Light Mode

Tap the toggle switch in the top right corner to switch between dark and light mode.
Your preference is saved and remembered the next time you open the app.

---

## Pull to Refresh

On your phone, pull down from the top of the Inventory page to refresh the list.
A teal spinning icon appears when you have pulled far enough — release to trigger the refresh.

---

## Installing on Your Phone

Runway-519 works like a native app with no App Store required.

**iPhone — use Safari:**

1. Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up)
2. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
3. Tap **Add**

**Android — use Chrome:**

1. Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
2. Tap **Add to Home Screen** or **Install App**

The Runway-519 icon appears on your home screen and opens fullscreen — no browser bar.
